About Us

CSR is the integration of socially beneficial programs and practices into a corporation's business model and culture. UPES ACM believes in giving back to the world around. We establish activities can help forge a stronger bond between employee, corporation and the outer world, this boost their morale and feel more connected with the world around them. UPES ACM aims to increase long-term relationships by enabling them to become more efficient and attract positive attention for their efforts. Basically this means “Good company, Good people, Good environment.”

Our Mission

It is our mission to Increase the integration of sustainability into business models and management of efforts. Engage with more institutions about policy to drive the global agenda of poverty, education and self-improvement among each other and leveraging plans with the society. Be a network which is recognised as a global leader. Provide learning aids and innovation opportunities to the global world to support their transformation and collaboration with us. It should be a clear way ahead because within the origin of the mission lie the ideals of the founder of the organisations.

Our Plan

The chapter conducted valuable CSR activities to reinforce its idea of sustainability. We believe in taking care of health and guaranteeing the best within us. Promoting the role and quality of giving back to the society. Our state is filled with developmental sessions to encourage and motivate the society into believing that they too can DREAM BIG. Integrity, Respect and Recognition, Passion for Excellence this is what lies within us.

Our Vision

A vision, a clear perception of what the company wants to achieve in the medium and long run. We believe that CSR must also make itself explicit in a series of promises and public commitments which must not be broken or forgotten, because it means a framework of actions for correct and efficient decision making, that’s what is remembered!

Be a part of what we call a world of “humanitarian”.

Hour of Code

Hour of Code was held on 9th December at Army Public School, Birpur, where we interacted with the students of 6th and 7th standard, the group of students who were eager to learn new things and could really understand the importance of computers and computing, starting from the 'Why to the "How They were acknowledged with introduction to Computers, their evolution, the current scenario, as well as the future course the domain is concerned. Interactive sessions based on basic coding on C/C++, and inculcate the idea of logical thinking and problem resolution through a plethora of puzzles and riddles were delineated. Later "Practical implementation of Java' was stated to 11th standard students. At last but not the least an entertainment session, including musical symphonies was held for amusement and enjoyment

Teach Girls

NO ONE IS CHERISHED MORE IN THE WORLD THAN SOMEONE WHO LIGHTENS THE BURDEN OF ANOTHER UPES ACM and UPES ACM-W student chapter believe to work for a cause and not for applause. The CSR heads with their team, full of energy and enthusiasm brought this into action by organizing a CSR activity. After the celebration of Women's Day week, our CSR heads decided to help the girls of TEACH GIRLS foundation, to grow with knowledge in innovatory and artistic ways. From presenting them a self-designed souvenir of white flowers inked with food colour to demonstrating the eruption of volcano, they gained awareness on optics, by learning and the formation of rainbows and creating mini microscopes with the help of CD's and paper cups respectively and the team ended with explaining significant information on pressure with the use of sealed bags full of water. The day also included little angels dancing with our team and a girl showcasing her talent of yoga. CSR team of the chapter will visit them again and inspire these children to focus on the greater good.
